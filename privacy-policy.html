Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

PIC Weather is an operational weather and mission-support application for drone pilots.

Data Collection

The app may use the device location to provide weather, mission, map, and operational risk information. Location access is used only to support the app's core functionality.

Weather Data

The app may connect to external weather data providers to retrieve forecasts, wind, gusts, precipitation, visibility, humidity, pressure, sunrise, sunset, and other environmental information.

Stored Data

The app may store mission points, selected locations, alert preferences, checklist status, and app settings locally on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party weather or map data services to provide weather and location-based information.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored locally on the device can be removed by deleting the app or clearing app data where supported.

Important Notice

PIC Weather supports operational planning. The pilot remains responsible for checking official weather sources, local aviation regulations, airspace restrictions, and final flight safety conditions before every mission.
